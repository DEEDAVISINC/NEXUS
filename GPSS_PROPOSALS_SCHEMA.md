## üéØ **RECOMMENDED VIEWS:**

### **View 1: Active Proposals**
- Filter: Status = Draft, Review, Ready to Send, Sent, Under Review
- Sort: Due Date (ascending)

### **View 2: Sent - Awaiting Response**
- Filter: Status = Sent OR Under Review
- Sort: Sent Date (oldest first)

### **View 3: Won Proposals**
- Filter: Outcome = Won
- Sort: Decision Date (descending)

### **View 4: Need Follow-up**
- Filter: Follow-up Required = Checked
- Filter: Next Follow-up Date ‚â§ Today
- Sort: Next Follow-up Date (ascending)

### **View 5: By Agency** (Group View)
- Group by: Agency Name
- Sort: Due Date (ascending)

### **View 6: This Month**
- Filter: Generated Date is within this month
- Sort: Generated Date (descending)
