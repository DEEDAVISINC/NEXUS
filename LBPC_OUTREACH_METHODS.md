## ğŸ’¡ PRO TIPS FOR EACH METHOD

### **Mail Tips:**
1. **Use Certified Mail for high-value leads** ($20K+)
   - Costs $8 but provides proof of delivery
   - Shows you're serious (not mass mailing)

2. **Hand-address envelopes** (or use handwriting font)
   - Looks personal, not bulk mail
   - Higher open rate

3. **Use real stamps** (not metered mail)
   - Looks more personal
   - Slightly higher open rate

4. **Include "TIME SENSITIVE" on envelope**
   - Creates urgency
   - Higher open rate

5. **Yellow paper** stands out
   - Some LBPC pros print on light yellow paper
   - Differentiates from white bills/junk mail

---

### **Email Tips:**
1. **Use their name in subject line**
   - "[Name], $18,500 waiting for you"
   - Higher open rate

2. **Keep it SHORT** (3-4 paragraphs max)
   - People skim emails
   - Get to the point fast

3. **Use plain text** (not fancy HTML)
   - Looks more personal
   - Less likely to be flagged as spam

4. **Include case number** for verification
   - Shows legitimacy
   - Easy for them to verify

5. **Add P.S. with urgency**
   - People always read the P.S.
   - Restate deadline

6. **Send from personal email** (not generic@company.com)
   - yourname@company.com feels more personal
   - Better deliverability

---

### **Phone Tips:**
1. **Call Tuesday-Thursday, 5-7pm**
   - Best time to reach working adults
   - After dinner but before bed

2. **Smile while you talk**
   - They can hear it in your voice
   - Sounds warmer and friendlier

3. **Stand up during calls**
   - More energy in your voice
   - Better delivery

4. **Use their name 3 times**
   - "Hi [Name]..."
   - "So [Name], here's what I recommend..."
   - "Great, [Name], I'll send that right over"

5. **Let them talk**
   - Ask questions, then LISTEN
   - Don't interrupt
   - Address their specific concerns

6. **Leave detailed voicemail** (if no answer)
   ```
   "Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from Lancaster Banques calling 
   about $[Amount] in surplus funds from [Property]. You can verify 
   this by calling [County] at [Phone], case number [Case #]. I'll 
   try you again tomorrow, or call me back at [Your Number]. Again, 
   that's [Your Number]. Thanks!"
   ```

7. **Follow up voicemail with text** (if you have cell)
   ```
   "Hi [Name], just left you a voicemail about $[Amount] in 
   surplus funds. Call me: [Phone] - [Your Name]"
   ```
