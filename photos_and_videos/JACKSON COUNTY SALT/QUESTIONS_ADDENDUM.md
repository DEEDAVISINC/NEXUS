## Strategic Implications

### Timeline Advantage
- Contract starts March 22, 2026
- Delivery not needed until October/November 2026
- **We have 8-9 months to secure supply**

### Competitive Landscape
- Jackson County knows about shortage
- They're willing to wait and see who bids
- We appear to be first to raise sourcing questions
- This may mean light competition or well-prepared competitors

### Bidding Strategy Options

**Option 1: Aggressive Sourcing + Competitive Bid**
- Lock down supplier NOW (even at higher price)
- Bid competitively knowing we have guaranteed supply
- Differentiate on reliability, not just price

**Option 2: Conditional Bid**
- Submit bid with supply contingency clause
- Price based on securing supply within 30 days of award
- Risk: May not be accepted

**Option 3: Partner with Established Supplier**
- Find supplier who has allocation secured
- Bid as their distributor/broker
- Smaller margin but guaranteed contract
