## üìã DETAILED SPECIFICATIONS:

### **MULCH REQUIREMENTS:**

**For Shrubs/Groundcover/Tree Rings:**
- 2 inches of new organic, shredded, composted mulch
- Maintain 2-inch layer at all times
- Must be completed before May 31
- Use "Doughnut method" around trees

**For Flower Beds:**
- 4 inches of new organic, shredded, composted mulch
- Remove all but 2 inches of existing mulch first
- Maintain 4-inch layer at all times
- Must be completed before May 31
- Color determined at installation

### **SEMI-MONTHLY BED MAINTENANCE** (May-October):
- Remove all leaves and debris
- Hand weed removal
- Chemical weed control
- Keep areas free of weeds
- Maintain clean appearance
- **Frequency:** 2 visits per month √ó 6 months = 12 visits per location

### **HERBICIDE APPLICATION:**
- Pre-emergence herbicide
- 2 applications annually: May and August
- Apply to all planter beds

### **PRUNING:**
- Ornamental trees in raised flower beds
- All shrubs and groundcover
- 2 times annually: June and September
- Maintain natural plant appearance

### **FALL CLEANUP:**
- Clean and clear all leaves and debris
- Prune and prepare plants for winter
- Complete before winter weather
