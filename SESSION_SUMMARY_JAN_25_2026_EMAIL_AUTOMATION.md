## üí° KEY STRATEGIC INSIGHTS

### **Insight #1: Bundle Strategy (Livonia)**
When you have multiple bids from the same organization with the same deadline, bundle them! Position as "single-source supplier" to increase win probability. Most competitors will bid only one product.

### **Insight #2: Diversity Pre-Qualification**
Before investing 8-10 hours in a bid, ASK if diversity certifications are valued. If "price only" on commodity products, consider skipping to focus on higher-probability opportunities.

### **Insight #3: Automation ROI**
Email automation saves 4-6 hours/week and allows you to review 5√ó more opportunities. The $5-16/month cost is negligible compared to the time value ($1,600-2,400/month) and increased win rate.

### **Insight #4: Your Sweet Spot**
You win when:
- Diversity is valued (EDWOSB/WOSB/MBE/WBE)
- Local proximity matters (Troy-based)
- Contract size is $20K-$200K
- Service/quality valued over pure price
- CDBG or federal funding (diversity requirements)

**Avoid:**
- Pure price competition on commodities
- Mega-contracts ($1M+) with thin margins
- Established suppliers with volume pricing you can't beat
