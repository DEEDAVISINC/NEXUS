## ‚ö†Ô∏è KEY RISKS TO MANAGE

### Risk 1: Supplier Availability
**Issue:** What if your supplier runs out?  
**Mitigation:** Have 2-3 backup suppliers lined up

### Risk 2: 24-48 Hour Delivery
**Issue:** Can your supplier guarantee this?  
**Mitigation:** Confirm delivery times in writing, have local supplier

### Risk 3: Price Volatility
**Issue:** Chlorine prices could rise (you're locked in 2 years)  
**Mitigation:** Build contingency into pricing (3-5%)

### Risk 4: Quantity Fluctuation
**Issue:** They might order less than 13,000 gallons  
**Mitigation:** Price per gallon protects you (no minimum)

### Risk 5: Seasonal Cash Flow
**Issue:** Deliveries may be seasonal (summer-heavy)  
**Mitigation:** Plan for uneven revenue throughout year
