ALEXIS NEXUS Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

1. Introduction

DEE DAVIS INC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the ALEXIS NEXUS Alexa skill ("the Skill"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your information when you use the Skill.

2. Information We Collect

When you use ALEXIS NEXUS, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Voice Interactions: Your voice commands and queries to the Skill
  • Business Data: Project information, contract details, financial metrics, task lists, and other business intelligence you request
  • Account Information: Name, email address, and authentication credentials
  • Usage Data: Intent names, timestamps, and interaction patterns to improve the Skill
  • Device Information: Alexa device type and identifier for service delivery

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide executive assistant functionality, business intelligence, and respond to your voice commands
  • Personalization: To learn your preferences and provide increasingly relevant assistance
  • System Integration: To connect with your NEXUS backend systems (GPSS, ATLAS, DDCSS, VERTEX, GBIS, LBPC)
  • AI Learning: To improve response accuracy and proactive insights over time
  • Security: To authenticate requests and protect your business data
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve the Skill

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Amazon Alexa: Voice interactions are processed by Amazon's Alexa service in accordance with Amazon's Privacy Policy
  • AWS Services: Data is stored and processed using Amazon Web Services (AWS Lambda, DynamoDB) with encryption
  • Your NEXUS Backend: Commands are routed to your private NEXUS backend systems for execution
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights

We do not share your business intelligence data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • JWT (JSON Web Token) authentication for all API requests
  • Data encryption at rest using AWS encryption services
  • Data encryption in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • AWS IAM least-privilege access controls
  • Secure credential storage in AWS Secrets Manager
  • Regular security audits and monitoring

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Skill's services:

  • Interaction Logs: Stored in DynamoDB for AI learning; retained for 12 months
  • Business Data: Stored in your NEXUS backend according to your retention policies
  • Session Data: Temporary session data is deleted after 24 hours of inactivity

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we have collected about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data by disabling the Skill
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the Skill at any time by saying "Alexa, disable ALEXIS NEXUS"
  • Data Export: Request export of your interaction logs

8. Children's Privacy

ALEXIS NEXUS is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

9. Third-Party Services

The Skill integrates with the following services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Amazon Alexa: Amazon Privacy Policy
  • Amazon Web Services: AWS Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude API: For AI-powered responses and analysis

10. International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers and service providers are located. By using the Skill, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection rules.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the Skill after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

13. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Our legal basis for processing your data is your consent and/or performance of a contract to provide the Skill's services.
