## üéØ **CERTIFICATIONS YOU MAY QUALIFY FOR**

### **1. Women-Owned Small Business (WOSB)**

**Qualifications:**
- ‚úÖ Business must be at least 51% owned by one or more women
- ‚úÖ Women must control day-to-day operations
- ‚úÖ Women must make long-term decisions
- ‚úÖ Must be a small business (varies by NAICS code)

**Where to Certify:**
- **SBA WOSB Program** (FREE): https://certify.sba.gov/
- **WBENC** (Women's Business Enterprise National Council): https://www.wbenc.org/
- **State/Local Programs**: Many states have their own certification

**Timeline:** 30-90 days  
**Cost:** $0 (SBA) or $350-500 (WBENC)

**Benefits:**
- Access to WOSB set-aside contracts (sole-source up to $7M)
- Corporate supplier diversity programs
- Competitive advantage in evaluations
- Networking opportunities

---

### **2. Economically Disadvantaged WOSB (EDWOSB)**

**Qualifications:**
- ‚úÖ Meet all WOSB requirements PLUS:
- ‚úÖ Owner's personal net worth less than $750,000 (excluding home and business)
- ‚úÖ Adjusted gross income averaged less than $350,000 over 3 years
- ‚úÖ Total assets less than $6 million

**Where to Certify:**
- **SBA EDWOSB Program** (FREE): https://certify.sba.gov/

**Timeline:** 30-90 days  
**Cost:** FREE

**Benefits:**
- All WOSB benefits PLUS:
- Access to EDWOSB-only set-asides
- Even stronger competitive advantage
- Priority in government scoring

---

### **3. Minority-Owned Business Enterprise (MBE)**

**Qualifications:**
- ‚úÖ Business must be at least 51% owned by minority individuals
- ‚úÖ Minorities must control operations and decisions
- ‚úÖ Owner must be U.S. citizen or legal resident

**Qualifying Minorities:**
- African American
- Hispanic American
- Native American
- Asian-Pacific American
- Subcontinent Asian American

**Where to Certify:**
- **NMSDC** (National Minority Supplier Development Council): https://nmsdc.org/
- **State/Local MBE Programs**

**Timeline:** 60-120 days  
**Cost:** $350-750

**Benefits:**
- Access to MBE set-asides
- Corporate supplier diversity programs
- Preference in evaluations
- Extensive corporate connections

---

### **4. Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (DBE)**

**Qualifications:**
- ‚úÖ Small business
- ‚úÖ Owner must be socially and economically disadvantaged
- ‚úÖ At least 51% owned by disadvantaged individuals
- ‚úÖ Personal net worth less than $1.32 million (excluding home and business)

**Where to Certify:**
- **State DOT Programs** (varies by state)
- Required for transportation-related contracts

**Timeline:** 90-180 days  
**Cost:** $0-200 (varies by state)

**Benefits:**
- Required for federally-assisted transportation projects
- 10% of DOT contracts reserved for DBEs
- Airport, transit, highway projects

---

### **5. 8(a) Business Development Program**

**Qualifications:**
- ‚úÖ Owner must be socially and economically disadvantaged
- ‚úÖ Business in operation for 2+ years
- ‚úÖ Personal net worth less than $850,000
- ‚úÖ Business revenue less than $1M (averaged over 3 years)
- ‚úÖ U.S. citizen

**Where to Certify:**
- **SBA 8(a) Program**: https://www.sba.gov/federal-contracting/contracting-assistance-programs/8a-business-development-program

**Timeline:** 90-180 days  
**Cost:** FREE

**Benefits:**
- 9-year program with mentorship
- Sole-source contracts up to $4M (services) or $7M (manufacturing)
- Set-aside competitions
- Business development support
- Training and counseling

---

### **6. Small Business Certification**

**Qualifications:**
- ‚úÖ Must meet SBA size standards for your NAICS code
- For most service businesses: Less than $8M-$41.5M annual revenue
- For NAICS 541690 (Consulting): Less than $30M annual revenue

**Where to Certify:**
- **SAM.gov**: Self-certify when registering
- **SBA Size Standards**: https://www.sba.gov/size-standards/

**Timeline:** Immediate  
**Cost:** FREE

**Benefits:**
- Required for any federal contracting
- Access to small business set-asides
- 23% of all federal contracts reserved
