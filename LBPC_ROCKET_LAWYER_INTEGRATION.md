## **ğŸ”§ TROUBLESHOOTING**

### **Common Issues:**

#### **"Document didn't copy to clipboard"**
**Solution:**
- Browser may have blocked clipboard access
- Click "Copy Again" button in modal
- Grant clipboard permission when prompted

#### **"Rocket Lawyer didn't open"**
**Solution:**
- Browser may have blocked pop-up
- Click "Open Rocket Lawyer" link in modal
- Allow pop-ups from LBPC domain

#### **"Can't find document in Rocket Lawyer"**
**Solution:**
- Create blank document first
- Then paste content
- Or use "My Documents" to find saved templates

#### **"Client hasn't signed yet"**
**Solution:**
- Check client's email address was correct
- Check their spam folder
- Adobe Sign allows you to resend reminder
- Can also text/call to notify them

#### **"E-signature not working in Rocket Lawyer"**
**Solution:**
- Ensure you clicked "Send for Signature" (not just "Share")
- Adobe Sign must be enabled in RL account
- Check RL account is active and paid
